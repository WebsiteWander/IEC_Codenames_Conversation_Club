About Us

About Us
This is IEC's Codenames Conversation Club

Our focus is to help Internation Students learn English. And what better way to learn English than playing a fun game? Using the game Codenames, we can encourage internation students to practice their English in a fun, easygoing environment.

We will have weekly games, hosted and assisted by the IEC Interns. Each winning team will have their pictures posted in the Hall of Fame page (click the link below) until the next game starts.
